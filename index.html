About

The person
behind the numbers

I began my career at Morgan Stanley's Private Wealth Management division, where I quickly distinguished myself by cultivating relationships with some of New York's most prominent family offices and entrepreneurs. My approach was always relational first, transactional second.

Today, as a Senior Advisor, I specialize in structuring complex, multi-generational wealth strategies — from alternative investments and private credit facilities to philanthropic giving vehicles and succession planning.

I hold an MBA from Wharton, am a CFA charterholder, and a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ professional. Beyond the credentials, I bring clarity, candor, and an obsession with client outcomes.
